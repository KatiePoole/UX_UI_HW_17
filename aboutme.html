About
Me

Hello, I'm Katie, a UX researcher & designer. I specialise in user-centric design, ensuring the user comes first and foremost when designing solutions.

I am a UX researcher & designer with a strong background in psychology and a deep passion for user research. Armed with a psychology degree, I bring a unique perspective to my design process, understanding the intricacies of human behaviour and cognition. 

My passion for user research fuels my desire to uncover valuable insights and empathise with users, enabling me to create designs that truly resonate with their needs and desires. I strive to understand user motivations and translate them into intuitive and user-centered digital experiences. With a blend of psychology, user research, and design skills, I am dedicated to crafting exceptional experiences that leave a lasting impact.
